Introduction John Cena is an American professional wrestler, actor, and TV show host. He’s most notoriously known for wrestling in the WWE; a career that he’s has since 2001. Other than the WWE, Cena has also starred in various films, including Trainwreck, Sisters, Daddy’s Home, and 12 Rounds.

Early Life

John Cena was born in West Newbury, Massachusetts, on the 23rd April, 1977. He is the second oldest of five siblings, all men. Cena attended Central Catholic High School in Lawrence, before making a transfer to Cushing Academy, a private prep boarding school. The TOP 5 Success Lessons from Eminem After graduating from the academy, he attended Springfield College, and was a NCAA Division III All-American center on the college football team. During this time, he wore the number 54 on the back of his shirt, which you may notice is often used on his WWE gear. Once Cena had graduated from college, he went and pursued bodybuilding as a career, whilst working as a limousine driver.

Career

John Cena's Career

John Cena began training to become a professional wrestler at the age of 22, at the Ultimate University based in California. A year later, he held the Ultimate Pro Wrestling Heavyweight Championship Title for 27 days in a row. In 2001, he left the UPW, and moved on to sign a contract with the WWF (now WWE). Within the next 10 years after that, he would rise to stardom within the WWE, and become one of the most popular wrestlers of all time. Aside from being a wrestler, John Cena has also starred in several movies; a couple of which are mentioned below, acting with the likes of Mark Wahlberg, Bill Hader, Amy Schumer, and Will Ferrell. As of 2021, John Cena’s net worth is estimated to be $60 million.

How Does John Cena Spend His Money?

When it comes to his cars, Cena has one 2006 Rolls Royce Phantom. Back to cars, Cena also has a Ferarri 599, he owns two of them as well. The Ferrari 599 is an expensive car to own, clocking in at $273,845. He also has a private jet which he uses when he wants to travel to see different WWE events. Cena is a huge proponent to using his money to give money to charitable causes. One of the organizations he’s been giving to is the Make A Wish Foundation. Cena’s mansion is in Tampa, Florida. He bought the property for $3.4 million.
